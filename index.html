ABOUT SMART INDUSTRY HACKATHON

[ IN COLLABORATION, THE CHENNAI INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY AND BONFIGLIOLI ] are hosting the Smart Industry 4.0 Hackathon 2024. This event will highlight cutting-edge advancements in manufacturing, smart technologies, and digital transformation. Drawing inspiration from the visionary legacy of Clementino Bonfiglioli and the innovative contributions of G.D. Naidu, the hackathon seeks to leverage their enduring creativity and practical ingenuity to address contemporary industrial challenges.
